Rasasi Hawas Her

Floral fragrance for women. Hawas for Her was launched in 2015. Hawas for Her was created by Claire Liégent and Dominique Ropion. Top notes are Pomegranate, Apple and Grapefruit. Middle notes are Iris, Jasmine Sambac and Citruses. Base notes are Praline, Patchouli and Vetiver.
