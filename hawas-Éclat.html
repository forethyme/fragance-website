Rasasi Hawas Éclat

Oriental Floral fragrance for women. This is a new fragrance. Hawas For Her Éclat was launched in 2025. The nose behind this fragrance is Amandine Galliano. Top notes are Litchi, Pear, Bergamot and Pistachio; middle notes are Rose and Incense; base notes are Vanilla, Musk, Amber and Woodsy Notes.
